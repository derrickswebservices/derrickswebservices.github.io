Frequently Asked Questions

Do I have to pay for hosting?

Regular websites are hosted for free under my domain. Only sites requiring a separate database/server, or your own custom domain have extra monthly costs. You can request a custom domain by emailing me.

What happens if I don't pay?

Sites with overdue payments are taken offline immediately. To restore the site, the balance must be paid in full.

Can I update my site later?

Yes. I offer maintenance plans (weekly or monthly) to handle updates for you at a low rate.

What if I aleady have a site, and just want an update or redesign?

Please email me directly to recieve a quote for these requests, and I will gladly help out.
